Data Processing

PDFPea processes all PDF files locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device and are not uploaded to our servers or any third-party services. All editing operations happen entirely on your local machine.

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views, browser type, and general location data. We do not collect personal information, email addresses, or any data that could identify individual users.

No File Storage

We do not store, backup, or retain any PDF files or documents you edit. All files remain on your device throughout the editing process and are permanently deleted from browser memory when you close the application.
